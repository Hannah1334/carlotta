Appearance

While she is pretty average in .. uh.. feminine attributes, her defined hourglass figure does a fine job proving she's female on its own. Very dark brown--almost black--hair falls down to her mid-back in gentle waves, framing an oval shaped face with delicate features. Pronounced cheekbones, a little slightly upturned button nose, full pink lips, and dark blue eyes. She's often found wearing pastels and pale colors, making her look a little darker than she really is.

Personality

To those whose company she enjoys, she's very charismatic and open minded, often showering her friends and loved ones in affection. She's a good listener but can also speak her own mind if the need arises. To those she doesn't like very much she can be snarky and sassy, using dry humor to get under their skin. Those people aren't very common, though, considering she's quite the people person and generally adores strangers long before they're her friends.
